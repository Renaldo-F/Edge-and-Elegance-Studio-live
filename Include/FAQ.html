Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our studio, services, and booking process.

What services do you offer?

We offer haircuts, beard grooming, braiding, nail care, and hair treatments — each crafted with precision and care by our expert team.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, but we recommend booking online to avoid waiting times. Walk-ins are welcome based on stylist availability.

Can I book online?

Yes, our platform allows you to book appointments quickly and easily. Select your preferred service, date, and time in just a few steps.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.
